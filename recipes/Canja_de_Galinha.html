Canja de Galinha

Description:

Let's head to the kitchen to prepare a Portuguese chicken soup? This recipe with free-range chicken is considered a medicinal dish, recommended for curing colds and flu. It's great to serve for dinner as it's the typical comfort food that helps you recover after a day of work or warms you up on a cold day.

Ingredients:

  • 1 chicken
  • 150g of macaroni
  • 1 and 1/2 liters of water
  • 1 onion
  • 1 tea spoon of pepper
  • 1/2 tea spoon of salt

Process:

  1. In a regular pot or pressure cooker, place the seasonings for this homemade chicken soup recipe: the onion cut in half or four pieces, the peppercorns, the salt, and if you wish, the chicken broth as well.
  2. Add the free-range chicken cut into halves or several pieces and cover it with water. Bring it to medium heat, cover, and let it cook until the chicken is ready.
  3. After cooking the chicken, remove it from the pot and shred it. Strain the chicken broth to remove the pieces of onion and peppercorns, then return it to the stove. Add the pasta and let it cook for the time indicated on the packaging.
  4. Finally, add the shredded chicken back into the pot, taste, and adjust the flavor of this comforting chicken soup.
  5. Turn off the heat and serve this delicious homemade free-range chicken soup immediately, garnished with boiled egg or parsley. Enjoy!
